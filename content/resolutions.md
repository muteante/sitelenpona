### January 
Supermajority resolution passed 2026-01-14, legally binding: **Remove Madame Lang as Facilitator/Director/President.**  


Supermajority resolution introduced  2026-01-17 failed, on the issue of approving new set of By-Laws.

### February

### March 

Official resolution passed 2025-03-13, binding: **Prepare to register the association under the *Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act.***

Official resolution passed 2025-03-14, binding: **Settle the translations for the terms Commons Sitelen Pona and Creative Sitelen Pona** as sitelen pona kulupu and sitelen pona musi.

Supermajority resolution passed 2025-03-17, binding: **Approve Unicode Proposal Synthesizer** methodology. 

Supermajority resolution passed 2025-03-17, binding: **Settle the Association's English and Toki Pona names** as Sitelen Pona Publishers and Typographers Association and kulupu pali pi sitelen pona. 

Supermajority resolution passed 2025-03-18, binding: **Amend Association By-Laws** that the threshold for Supermajority resolutions be set at 70% of participating voting members.

Official resolution passed 2025-03-25, binding: **Settle the Association's French name** as <span lang="fr">Association de l’édition et de la typographie du sitelen pona</span>

Supermjajority resolution passed 2025-03-28, binding: **Settle the style of the Association's logo** from a set of proposed options. 

### April 

Official resolution passed 2025-04-12, binding: **Publish drafted content to public website**

Official resolution passed 2025-04-15, binding: **Settle translation for the term ‘Rare Sitelen Pona’** as *sitelen pona pi kepeken lili* should such a term be required.


Official resolution passed 2025-04-18, binding: **Settle luka+luka+luka+luka and directional ni as combined glyphs** instead of dedicated codepoints. 


### May 

Official resolution passed 2025-05-30, binding: **Settle the Association’s registered legal name** in English as Sitelen Pona Publishers and Typographers Association (kulupu pali pi sitelen pona) and in French as Association de l’édition et de la typographie du sitelen pona (kulupu pali pi sitelen pona)

### June

Official resolution passed 2025-06-04, non-binding due to misformulation: **Settle the exclusion of Latin-style period** from Common Sitelen Pona as without objection.
